Detailed Description

////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// ////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// ////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// Warped disk in 3d: zeta[0]=x; zeta[1]=y (so it doesn't have coordinate singularities), with specification of two boundaries (b=0,1) that turn the whole thing into a circular disk.
