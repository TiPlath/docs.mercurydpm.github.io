class Eigen::internal::group_theory::dimino_add_cosets_for_rep< Multiply, Equality, id, sub_group_elements, elements, generators, rep_element, sub_group_size >

Recursive template for adding coset spaces.

This template multiplies the coset representative with a generator from the list of previous generators. If the new element is not in the group already, it adds the corresponding coset. Finally it proceeds to call itself with the next generator from the list.
