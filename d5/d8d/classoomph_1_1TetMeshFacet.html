Detailed Description

///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// ///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// ///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// Facet for Tet mesh generation. Can lie on boundary (identified via one-based enumeration!) and can have GeomObject associated with those boundaries.
