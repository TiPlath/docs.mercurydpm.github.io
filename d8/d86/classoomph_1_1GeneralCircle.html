Detailed Description

//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// //////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// //////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// GeneralCircle in 2D space.

\[ x = X_c + R \cos(\zeta) \]

\[ y = Y_c + R \sin(\zeta) \]

The three parameters \( X_c, Y_c \) and \( R \) are represented by Data and can therefore be unknowns in the problem.
